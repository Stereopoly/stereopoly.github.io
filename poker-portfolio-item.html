Project Description

An app developed for my Algebra 2 Honors class final project with 4 others. The app performs millions calculations to present the user probabilities of achieving a certain hand. Originally, these calculations took about 2 minutes, but after many opimizations including representing poker cards using bits rather than objects, we could get it down to under 5 seconds.
