Hackathons

Creative innovation in teams

Organizing and Participating

I love to participate in hackathons, working with other creative people to create fun pieces of software and possibly win prizes. Being captive for 24-36 hours in one location and having food and everything dealt with creates a highly efficient environment. Some hackathons I have attended include HackingEDU, Codeday, and Los Altos Hacks I.

In addition to participating in hackathons, I am also on the organizing team for a hackathon called Los Altos Hacks. 2018 will be the third iteration of this hackathon inviting high schoolers from around the Bay Area.
