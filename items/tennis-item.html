History

I started playing tennis at just 7 years old at Cuesta Park, but it didn't fully click with me until I joined the Blach Middle School tennis team with a bunch of my friends. At this point, I started playing much more seriously at a tennis club and on weekends with friends and family.

School

I currently represent my school on the Varsity tennis team, typically playing 1st doubles or low singles. Going to tennis practice everyday after school has been one of my high school highlights, especially the 3 day trip down to Fresno with the team. I hope to continue this hobby throughout my student career and for the rest of my life.
