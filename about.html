About Oscar Bjorkman

I am currently a student at UC Berkeley studying Computer Science who loves to use the power of technology. In 6th grade, I built my own powerful workstation for all of my coding and school needs. In 7th grade, I bought a course on Udemy and taught myself web development. I participate in robotics competitions, write iPhone applications, attend hackathons as time permits, and look for opportunities to work with cool technology. I currently have 6 apps on the Apple App Store used by tens of thousands of people across the world. When I'm away from HAL (my computer), I enjoy playing tennis, flying drones, and traveling. I can read and write Chinese, and speak some Swedish.
